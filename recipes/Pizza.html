Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Melted Butter

  • Steps

    1. In a bowl, whisk together 1 cup flour, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp baking powder, 1 egg, 1 cup milk, and 2 tbsp melted butter until smooth.
    2. Heat a lightly oiled skillet over medium heat and pour in 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
    3. Cook until bubbles form on top, flip, and cook until golden brown.
